our privacy policy

Asperger Media Pty Ltd (us) is subject to relevant laws in Australia (the National Privacy Principles and the Privacy Act 1988). This privacy statement explains how we use personal information that you provide to us, either by use of this website, or the procurement of services provided by us.

Asperger Media may, at its discretion, collect personal information if you engage us by email, phone, mail, carrier pigeon, or smoke signal. We do this to create a profile about you we can then use to store information about work we have done with - and for - you. Our interest is not to sell (or share) this information with a third-party, except where it may allow us to send you a marketing communication, or is required under law.

You are welcome to opt out of marketing communications by contacting us at hello(at)asperger.media. We endeavour to complete requests within one business day, at which time you'll recieve a confirmation email - You may also unsubscribe after recieving a marketing communication by clicking the unsubscribe link in the email.

Disclosure of Personal Information -

Asperger Media aims to ensure only relevant personal information (your first and last names and email), are shared for the purposes of sending out marketing communications.

Asperger Media may provide the full documented personal information of a client where it is required for legal or customs purposes.

Asperger Media will not disclose personal information without written consent other than for the above purposes, as necessary to prevent a threat to life or health, as authorised or required by law, as reasonably necessary to enforce the law, or as necessary to investigate any suspected unlawful activity.

Use of this website -

Asperger Media stores the following information on interaction with this website:

  • Your browser type
  • Your IP Address
  • The Operating System you operate
  • The pages you've accessed and documents (where relevant) you've downloaded
  • The date and time of your visit

This information is used to try to ensure compatibility with the most number of browsers and operating systems that we can. We also use this information for analytical purposes to establish how our website is used and what information is the most searched for. This allows us to make accessing this website easier for you.
