our services

labour charges and callout fees

Asperger Media charges on a 'parts and labour' basis. Parts are charged in advance, labour is charged after the job is complete.

If we agree to have a look at a product that we don't have a line-item for, we will charge it at our 'general' rate, at $60/hr. Labour is charged at $32.50/hr, or $75/hr.

If you need us to come out to you urgently, we will charge an emergency callout fee, at $120 per instance.
